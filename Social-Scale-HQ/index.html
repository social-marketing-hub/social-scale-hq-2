✦ Step by step
The Scale Roadmap 🗺️
10 steps to grow from zero to authority across all 5 platforms — in the right order, without overwhelm.
1
Lock in your niche & ideal audience 🎯
Before a single post: define exactly WHO you serve, WHAT problem you solve, and WHY you're the one to solve it. One sentence: "I help [who] achieve [what] by [how]."
2
Optimize all 5 profiles with one consistent identity ✨
Same profile photo, username (or close variant), bio formula: Hook + credibility + niche + CTA. Every profile should pass the "3-second test."
3
Build your 3–5 content pillars 🧱
Every post falls under one of 3–5 pillars. This creates the consistency algorithms love and audiences can predict. Go to the Pillars tab to build yours.
4
Master one short-form platform first 💅
Go all-in on IG Reels or TikTok for 30 days. Post daily. Study analytics. Find your top 3 performing formats. Then replicate and cross-post.
5
Build a repurpose system around one anchor piece ♻️
One YouTube video or long-form carousel per week → 1 IG Reel + 1 TikTok + 1 FB post + 3 Pinterest pins + Stories. One piece = 7–10 posts.
6
Obsess over hooks — your #1 growth lever 🎣
70% of your growth comes from hooks. The first 1–3 seconds determines if 1K or 100K people see your content. Test 3 hook styles weekly.
7
Collab & community growth phase 🤝
At 1K+: IG Collab posts, TikTok Duets & Stitches, YouTube collabs, Pinterest group boards. Each collab exposes you to a warm new audience instantly.
8
Add Pinterest as your evergreen SEO engine 📌
Pinterest pins gain traffic for months and years. Set up keyword-optimized boards, pin 10–15/day, and link all pins to your website or email opt-in.
9
Build YouTube as your authority anchor 👑
At 5K+ short-form followers, launch YouTube with long-form pillar videos. These rank on Google for years and turn viewers into true fans.
10
Convert followers to email subscribers 💌
Followers are rented. Email is owned. Drive people to a lead magnet at every growth phase. Your email list converts 10–40x better than social DMs.

Content Pillars 🧱
5 universal content pillars every social manager needs — adapted to any niche.
Why pillars matter 💡 Algorithms reward accounts that consistently create in specific themes. Pillars make planning fast, audience expectations clear, and posting sustainable. Pick 3–5 for your niche.
01
🎓 Educate & Add Value
Teach something actionable. How-tos, tips, tutorials, listicles. Builds authority and gets SAVED — the highest-value engagement signal on IG and Pinterest.
02
😂 Entertain & Relate
Humor, relatable moments, trends, personality-driven content. Drives SHARES — highest-value on TikTok and Facebook. People share what makes them feel seen.
03
💪 Inspire & Motivate
Transformations, behind-the-scenes journeys, aspirational content. SAVES and FOLLOWS spike when people feel "this could be me."
04
🤝 Build Community
Q&As, polls, collabs, UGC, audience spotlights. Drives COMMENTS — crucial for Facebook and IG algorithm. Community = loyalty = retention.
05
📣 Promote & Convert
Soft CTAs, product features, testimonials, offer reveals. Cap at 20% of total content. The other 4 pillars earn the right to promote.

📸 Instagram Playbook
The complete scaling guide for Instagram — the #1 discovery platform for most niches.
2025 IG Algorithm Priority: Reels reach > Carousels (saves) > Stories (daily engagement). Reels are still the primary growth lever for reaching new audiences.
Profile Optimization ✨
  • ♡ Username: simple, searchable, matches other platforms
  • ♡ Bio: Hook (who you help) + social proof + CTA
  • ♡ Link in bio → Linktree with 4–5 key links
  • ♡ Story Highlights: Intro | Best Posts | Services | Free Resource
Reels Growth Strategy 🎬
  • ♡ Hook in first 0–3 seconds — text overlay on screen
  • ♡ Post 1 Reel/day during growth phase
  • ♡ Use trending audio within 72 hours
  • ♡ Always add captions (85% watch muted)
  • ♡ End with CTA: "Follow for more" or "Save this"
Engagement Loops 💬
  • ♡ Reply to EVERY comment in the first 60 minutes
  • ♡ 15 min before/after posting engaging on niche accounts
  • ♡ Use Stories daily to stay top of followers' feeds
  • ♡ Collab posts = shared to both audiences simultaneously

👥 Facebook Playbook
Facebook is #1 for community-building, ad retargeting, and reaching the 35–60 demographic.
FB Algorithm 2025: Comments > Shares > Reactions. Ask questions. Build Groups. Repurpose Reels natively. FB Pages with active Groups get priority organic reach.
Page Strategy
  • ♡ Post 5× per week — mix native videos, text, Reels
  • ♡ Native video gets 10× more reach than YouTube links
  • ♡ Repost IG Reels natively (no watermark)
  • ♡ Longer captions work better on FB — storytelling approach
Group Strategy 🤝
  • ♡ Create a free community Group around your niche
  • ♡ Post 1 discussion prompt per day
  • ♡ Use the Group to test content ideas
  • ♡ Live videos inside Groups = enormous organic reach
Facebook Ads 💰
  • ♡ Boost top-performing organic posts ($5–$10/day)
  • ♡ Retarget video viewers with follow-up offers
  • ♡ Lookalike audiences based on email list
  • ♡ Test 3 creatives simultaneously, kill losers after 48 hrs
FB Content Mix 🎀
40% Educational/value posts
25% Entertainment/relatable content
20% Community/engagement posts
15% Promotional CTAs

🎵 TikTok Playbook
Master the For You Page and your content can reach millions — regardless of follower count.
TikTok FYP Algorithm: Completion rate > Shares > Comments > Likes. If people watch to the end and rewatch, TikTok pushes it to more people. Loops are king.
Growth Fundamentals ⚡
  • ♡ Post 1–3× per day — TikTok rewards volume
  • ♡ Keep videos under 30 sec for max completion rate
  • ♡ Hook must appear in first 0–2 seconds
  • ♡ Trending sounds within 48–72 hrs = wider FYP reach
  • ♡ NEVER post IG watermarked videos
TikTok-Specific Formats ✨
  • ♡ Stitch: React to viral video in your niche
  • ♡ Duet: Side-by-side collab with creators
  • ♡ Series: Multi-part content drives return viewers
  • ♡ TikTok Live: At 1K+ — massive organic visibility
  • ♡ Green Screen: Authority content format
Cross-Posting ♻️
  • ♡ Crosspost to IG Reels (remove TikTok watermark)
  • ♡ Save to YouTube Shorts
  • ♡ Pin your best TikTok to Pinterest as video pin
  • ♡ Repurpose top TikToks to Facebook Reels
Hashtag Strategy 🏷️
Use: 1–2 niche hashtags (under 500M views) + 1–2 mid hashtags (500M–1B views) + 1 broad hashtag (#fyp). Limit to 3–5 total. Relevance > volume.
✦ The #2 search engine in the world
▶️ YouTube Playbook
Your long-game authority builder — content ranks for years on YouTube and Google.
YouTube Algorithm: CTR + Watch time = distribution. A great thumbnail gets the click. Great content keeps them watching. Both are mandatory.
Channel Setup ✨
  • ♡ Channel art: communicate your niche in 5 words
  • ♡ Channel trailer: 60–90 sec "why subscribe" video
  • ♡ 3–4 playlists organized by subtopic from day one
  • ♡ Keywords in: channel name, about, titles, descriptions
Thumbnail & Title Formula 🎨
  • ♡ Thumbnail: face + bold 3–4 word text + contrast colors
  • ♡ Title: [Keyword] + [Benefit/Curiosity] under 60 chars
  • ♡ A/B test thumbnails via YouTube Studio
  • ♡ Target 6–10% CTR (over 10% = viral potential)
YouTube SEO 🔍
  • ♡ Research keywords in YouTube Search autocomplete
  • ♡ VidIQ or TubeBuddy free tiers for keyword volume
  • ♡ Put main keyword in first 25 words of description
  • ♡ Add chapters — increases watch time and SEO
Shorts Strategy ⚡
  • ♡ Shorts get shown to non-subscribers — great for discovery
  • ♡ Post 3–5 Shorts/week to grow before long-form
  • ♡ Repurpose TikToks and IG Reels as Shorts
  • ♡ Use Shorts to tease upcoming long-form videos

📌 Pinterest Playbook
Pinterest drives evergreen traffic for months and years. The gift that keeps on giving. 🌸
Pinterest's superpower: Unlike IG/TikTok posts that die in 24–48 hours, Pinterest pins generate traffic for 6+ months. One great pin = thousands of website visits over its lifetime.
Profile & Board Setup 🌷
  • ♡ Profile name: [Your Name] | [Primary Keyword]
  • ♡ Create 8–12 boards before pinning
  • ♡ Board names: exact keywords (not creative names)
  • ♡ Enable Rich Pins (verifies your website)
Pin Design 🎨
  • ♡ Vertical ratio: 2:3 (1000×1500px) performs best
  • ♡ Bold title text on the pin image
  • ♡ Consistent branding: your brand colors in Canva
  • ♡ Create 3–5 pin designs for the same content (A/B test)
  • ♡ Video pins (under 60 sec) are favored in feed
Pinning Schedule 📅
  • ♡ Pin 10–15× per day minimum — use Tailwind
  • ♡ Best times: evenings and weekends
  • ♡ Mix: 20% your content + 80% repinned content
  • ♡ Don't delete pins — every pin = backlink to your site
Traffic Loop 🔄
Step 1: Write a blog post on a keyword your audience searches.

Step 2: Create 5 pin designs for that post.

Step 3: Pin one per day to relevant boards.

Step 4: Blog post has an email opt-in.

Result: Pinterest → Blog → Email list 💌

✨ 1 Piece → 10+ Posts Workflow
Step 1 🎬 Create the Anchor
Record a 10–15 min YouTube video OR write a detailed blog post. Batch record 4 anchors in one session per month.
Step 2 ✂️ Extract Short-Form
Pull 3 "golden moments" for Reels/TikToks. Write a carousel from the main points. Create a Stories Q&A. = 5–6 pieces from 1 video.
Step 3 📌 Pinterest Amplify
Design 5 pin covers for the same blog post. Pin one per day across relevant boards. = 5 more posts, lasting months.
Step 4 👥 Facebook Repackage
Share video natively. Post carousel slides individually. Create a Group discussion thread. = 3 more posts, zero new creation.
