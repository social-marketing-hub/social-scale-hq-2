02 / Roadmap
ROADMAP
Ten steps to full-platform authority
A structured sequence to build from zero to authority across all five platforms — in the right order, without overwhelm.
01
Lock in your niche and ideal audience
Before a single post: define exactly who you serve, what problem you solve, and why you're the one to solve it. One sentence — "I help [who] achieve [what] by [how]."
02
Optimize all five profiles with one consistent identity
Same profile photo, username (or close variant), and bio formula: Hook + credibility + niche + CTA. Every profile should pass the three-second test.
03
Build your content pillars
Every post falls under one of three to five pillars. This creates the consistency algorithms favor and audiences can predict. Navigate to the Pillars section to build yours.
04
Master one short-form platform first
Go all-in on IG Reels or TikTok for 30 days. Post daily. Study analytics. Find your top three performing formats. Then replicate and cross-post.
05
Build a repurpose system around one anchor piece
One YouTube video or long-form carousel per week becomes one IG Reel, one TikTok, one FB post, three Pinterest pins, and Stories. One piece equals seven to ten posts.
06
Obsess over hooks — your primary growth lever
Seventy percent of your growth comes from hooks. The first one to three seconds determines whether a thousand or a hundred thousand people see your content. Test three hook styles weekly.
07
Enter the collab and community growth phase
At 1K+: IG Collab posts, TikTok Duets and Stitches, YouTube collaborations, Pinterest group boards. Each collab exposes you to a warm new audience instantly.
08
Activate Pinterest as your evergreen SEO engine
Pinterest pins gain traffic for months and years. Set up keyword-optimized boards, pin 10 to 15 per day, and link all pins to your website or email opt-in.
09
Build YouTube as your authority anchor
At 5K+ short-form followers, launch YouTube with long-form pillar videos. These rank on Google for years and convert viewers into genuine fans.
10
Convert followers to email subscribers
Followers are rented. Email is owned. Drive people to a lead magnet at every growth phase. Your email list converts ten to forty times better than social engagement alone.

04 / Content Pillars
PILLARS
The foundation of a scalable content system
Five universal content pillars for any niche. Algorithms reward thematic consistency. Pillars make planning fast, audience expectations clear, and output sustainable.
01
Pillar One
Educate and add value
Teach something actionable. How-tos, tips, tutorials, listicles. Builds authority and drives saves — the highest-value signal on IG and Pinterest.
02
Pillar Two
Entertain and relate
Humor, relatable moments, trends, personality-driven content. Drives shares — the highest-value signal on TikTok and Facebook.
03
Pillar Three
Inspire and motivate
Transformations, journeys, aspirational content. Saves and follows spike when an audience feels "this could be me."
04
Pillar Four
Build community
Q&As, polls, collabs, UGC, audience spotlights. Drives comments — crucial for Facebook and Instagram algorithms. Community equals loyalty.
05
Pillar Five
Promote and convert
Soft CTAs, product features, testimonials, offer reveals. Cap at 20% of total output. The other four pillars earn the right to promote.

06 / Instagram
INSTAGRAM
The primary discovery platform
The complete scaling guide for Instagram — the highest-leverage discovery platform for most niches in 2025.
2025 Algorithm Priority: Reels reach > Carousels (saves) > Stories (daily engagement). Reels remain the primary growth lever for reaching new audiences outside your following.
Profile Optimization
  • —Username: simple, searchable, consistent across platforms
  • —Bio: Hook (who you help) + social proof + CTA
  • —Link in bio via Linktree with four to five key links
  • —Story Highlights: Intro / Best Posts / Services / Free Resource
Reels Growth System
  • —Hook in the first zero to three seconds with text overlay
  • —Post one Reel per day during active growth phase
  • —Use trending audio within 72 hours of spotting it
  • —Always add captions — 85% watch with sound off
  • —End with a CTA: "Follow for more" or "Save this"
Engagement Protocol
  • —Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes after posting
  • —15 minutes of niche engagement before and after posting
  • —Use Stories daily to remain top of followers' feeds
  • —Collab posts share to both audiences simultaneously

07 / Facebook
FACEBOOK
Community and paid media powerhouse
Facebook is the leading platform for community-building, ad retargeting, and reaching the 35–60 demographic at scale.
Algorithm Priority: Comments > Shares > Reactions. Ask questions. Build Groups. Repurpose Reels natively. Pages with active Groups receive priority organic distribution.
Page Strategy
  • —Post five times per week — mix native video, text, and Reels
  • —Native video receives ten times the reach of YouTube links
  • —Repost IG Reels natively without watermark
  • —Longer captions perform better on FB — lead with storytelling
Group Strategy
  • —Create a free community Group around your niche
  • —Post one discussion prompt per day
  • —Use the Group to test and validate content ideas
  • —Live videos inside Groups generate enormous organic reach
Facebook Ads
  • —Boost top-performing organic posts at $5–$10 per day
  • —Retarget video viewers with a follow-up offer
  • —Build Lookalike audiences from your email list
  • —Test three creatives simultaneously; kill underperformers in 48 hours

08 / TikTok
TIKTOK
The fastest route to viral discovery
Master the For You Page and your content can reach millions — regardless of your current follower count.
FYP Algorithm: Completion rate > Shares > Comments > Likes. If viewers watch to the end and rewatch, TikTok amplifies to a larger audience. Loops are the metric that matters most.
Growth Fundamentals
  • —Post one to three times per day — TikTok rewards consistent volume
  • —Keep videos under 30 seconds for maximum completion rate
  • —Hook must appear in the first zero to two seconds
  • —Use trending sounds within 48–72 hours for wider FYP reach
  • —Never post IG-watermarked content — TikTok actively suppresses it
TikTok-Native Formats
  • —Stitch: React to viral content in your niche
  • —Duet: Side-by-side collaboration with creators
  • —Series: Multi-part content drives return viewers
  • —TikTok Live: At 1K+ — substantial organic visibility
  • —Green Screen: Authority content format for education
Cross-Platform Distribution
  • —Cross-post to IG Reels after removing TikTok watermark
  • —Save and upload to YouTube Shorts
  • —Pin your best TikTok to Pinterest as a video pin
  • —Repurpose top TikToks to Facebook Reels natively
Hashtag Strategy
Use one to two niche hashtags (under 500M views) plus one to two mid-tier hashtags (500M–1B views) plus one broad hashtag. Limit to three to five total. Relevance outperforms volume every time.
09 / YouTube
YOUTUBE
The world's second-largest search engine
Your long-game authority builder. Content ranks for years on YouTube and surfaces on Google — no other platform offers this compounding return.
Algorithm: CTR + Watch time = distribution. A great thumbnail earns the click. Great content keeps them watching. Both are non-negotiable.
Channel Setup
  • —Channel art: communicate your niche in five words or fewer
  • —Channel trailer: 60–90 seconds explaining why to subscribe
  • —Three to four playlists organized by subtopic from day one
  • —Keywords in channel name, about section, titles, and descriptions
Thumbnail and Title Formula
  • —Thumbnail: face + bold three-to-four word text + high contrast
  • —Title: [Keyword] + [Benefit or Curiosity] under 60 characters
  • —A/B test thumbnails via YouTube Studio
  • —Target 6–10% CTR — above 10% signals viral potential
YouTube SEO
  • —Research keywords using YouTube Search autocomplete
  • —VidIQ or TubeBuddy free tiers for keyword volume data
  • —Include main keyword in the first 25 words of description
  • —Add video chapters — increases watch time and search indexing
Shorts Strategy
  • —Shorts are shown to non-subscribers — ideal for discovery
  • —Post three to five Shorts per week to build before long-form
  • —Repurpose TikToks and IG Reels as Shorts
  • —Use Shorts to tease and link to upcoming long-form videos
10 / Pinterest
PINTEREST
The most underrated platform in the system
Pinterest drives evergreen traffic for months and years. Unlike posts on other platforms that expire in 48 hours, a great pin continues generating results indefinitely.
Pinterest's structural advantage: Content ranks and compounds over six-plus months. One high-quality pin can drive thousands of website visits over its lifetime — a metric no short-form platform can match.
Profile and Board Setup
  • —Profile name format: [Your Name] | [Primary Keyword]
  • —Create eight to twelve boards before pinning content
  • —Board names: exact search keywords, never creative titles
  • —Enable Rich Pins to verify your website with Pinterest
Pin Design Principles
  • —Vertical ratio: 2:3 (1000x1500px) — performs best in feed
  • —Bold title text on the pin image itself
  • —Consistent branding: use your brand colors in Canva
  • —Create three to five pin designs per piece of content for A/B testing
  • —Video pins under 60 seconds receive feed priority
Pinning Schedule
  • —Pin 10–15 times per day minimum — use Tailwind to schedule
  • —Best times: evenings and weekends across time zones
  • —Mix: 20% your own content, 80% repinned niche content
  • —Never delete pins — every pin is a backlink to your site
The Pinterest Traffic Loop
01Write a blog post targeting a keyword your audience searches
02Create five pin designs for that post
03Pin one per day to relevant boards over five days
04Blog post contains an email opt-in
ResultPinterest > Blog > Email list — on autopilot

Tool / Repurpose Engine
REPURPOSE
One piece, ten platforms
The most efficient content managers build systems around one anchor piece per week and distribute it everywhere. Create once — distribute everywhere.
The Repurpose Principle: Every piece of content should appear on at least three platforms. One YouTube video can generate ten or more pieces with zero additional recording time.

One Piece to Ten Posts — The Workflow
Step 01 — Create the Anchor
Record a 10–15 minute YouTube video or write a detailed blog post. Batch-record four anchors in a single session per month to maximize efficiency.
Step 02 — Extract Short-Form
Pull three strong moments for Reels and TikToks. Write a carousel from the main points. Create a Stories Q&A sequence. Five to six pieces from one video.
Step 03 — Amplify on Pinterest
Design five pin covers for the same blog post. Pin one per day across relevant boards. Five more posts, generating traffic for months.
Step 04 — Repackage for Facebook
Share the video natively. Post carousel slides individually. Open a Group discussion thread. Three more posts with zero new content creation.
